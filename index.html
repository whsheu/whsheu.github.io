    About me

    I am a second-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of California, Davis (UC Davis). I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Slobodan Mitrović.

    I received my Master's degree at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Taiwan, where I had the honor to be advised by Professor Biing-Feng Wang.

    Research interest: I am interested in combinatorial optimization and algorithmic graph theory, with an emphasis on the design and analysis of distributed algorithms and streaming algorithms.

    Contact: My email is wsheu at ucdavis dot edu
